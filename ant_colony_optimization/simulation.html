Ant Colony Optimization

Ant Colony Optimization is an interesting way to obtain near-optimum solutions to the Travelling Salesman Problem (TSP). It involves utilizing multi-agent ants to explore all possible solutions and converge upon a short path with a combination of a priori knowledge and pheromone trails deposited by other ants
